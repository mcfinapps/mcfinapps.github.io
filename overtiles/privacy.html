Privacy by design

Privacy policy

Last updated 20 July 2026

Overtiles is designed to be enjoyed entirely on your device. The app does not require an account or a network connection.

Data collection

Overtiles does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data. The app contains no advertising, analytics, tracking technologies or third-party software development kits that collect data.

On-device information

Your game progress, high scores, chosen themes and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to operate the app and is not available to McFin.

Network access

Overtiles does not connect to McFin servers or other online services. Any App Store purchase and download information is handled by Apple under Apple’s own privacy policy.

Children’s privacy

Because Overtiles collects no personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If the app’s privacy practices materially change, this page will be updated before the change is released.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to mike.mcfin@gmail.com.
