Two ways to play

Remember—or find your flow.

The same tactile atelier supports two distinctly different challenges. Your chosen material stays with you as you move between them.

01 · Memory

Find what belongs together.

Turn two tiles. Hold their places in mind. Match every pair in as few moves as you can—or simply settle into an unhurried ritual of discovery.

Observation · Recall · Reward

02 · Focus

Find the next number.

Every tile is visible. Begin at 00 and move through the sequence before time runs out. The board becomes quiet; attention becomes momentum.

Scan · Select · Flow

Two distinct challenges. The same beautifully physical material world.

Recall and rapid scanning are exactly the territory cognitive-training research has studied for decades — Overtiles just makes the practice worth returning to.

A real sense of depth

Turn the tile.
Feel its weight.

Each tile lifts, reveals a visible edge and settles with a restrained overshoot. Walnut turns with warmth, aluminium snaps into place, leather yields, basalt lands heavily and lacquer answers with a bright contact.
