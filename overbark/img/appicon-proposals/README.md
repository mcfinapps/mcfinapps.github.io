# Overbark app-icon proposals

All proposals are full-bleed, square `1024 × 1024` PNG masters without pre-rounded corners or text.

## Creative lead ranking

1. **Speaking terrier — pick.** The clearest promise at a glance: dog + voice. It has the strongest small-size silhouette, connects directly to the product, and carries the premium light palette without looking like a UI screenshot.
2. **Two-dog conversation.** The most emotionally distinctive and ownable alternative. It celebrates the two-dog feature, but can initially read as a pet-family app.
3. **Terrier O monogram.** Elegant and highly ownable, but the voice/translation purpose is less immediate and the negative-space letter needs refinement.
4. **Thoughtful terrier.** Warm and charming, but too close to a general pet or dog-care identity.

## Shared art direction

- Warm cream, ivory, smoked terracotta, espresso and muted teal.
- No wording, UI replica, waveform or pre-applied Apple corner mask.
- Bold, centered silhouettes designed to remain legible at notification and Spotlight sizes.
- These are concept renders. The selected direction should receive a final geometry pass and standard/dark/tinted appearance variants before App Store submission.
